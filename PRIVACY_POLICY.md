# Privacy Policy for SpaceDefender

**Last Updated:** April 10, 2026

## Introduction

SpaceDefender is a mobile game that respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any data when you use our app.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **No Personal Information:** SpaceDefender does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics or tracking services
- **No Location Data:** The app does not access your location
- **No Contacts:** The app does not access your contacts
- **No Camera/Microphone:** The app does not access your camera or microphone

### What We Store Locally
- **Game Progress:** Your high scores and game progress are stored locally on your device
- **Settings:** Game preferences and settings are stored locally on your device

## Data Usage

- All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any servers
- We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data
- We do not use third-party advertising services

## Data Security

- All game data is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted over the internet, eliminating security risks associated with data transmission

## Children's Privacy

- SpaceDefender is suitable for users of all ages
- We do not collect any personal information, including from children under 13

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document.
